A letter from our founder

For three decades, one promise — treat every patient like family.

When we opened our doors on Jefferson Avenue in 1994, Midland was a smaller town and dentistry looked very different. What hasn't changed is our approach: unhurried visits, honest recommendations, and a team that genuinely cares how you feel walking out the door.

Today, we've grown into a four-provider practice with the technology to handle almost anything in-house — but we still greet you at the front desk and remember your kids' names. That's the McLaren difference.

— Dr. Andrew Chen
Founder · Practice Principal

New patient visit

What to expect, start to finish.

Your first visit sets the foundation for everything that follows. Here's exactly how it goes — no surprises.

i.

A warm welcome

Arrive, meet your hygienist, and take a quick office tour. We'll verify your insurance so you leave knowing exactly what's covered.

~10 min
ii.

Gentle exam & imaging

Low-radiation digital X-rays and intraoral photos. Your dentist reviews them with you on screen — not at you.

~20 min
iii.

Cleaning & conversation

A thorough but gentle cleaning. We'll talk through what we see and what (if anything) you might want to address.

~30 min
iv.

Plan & transparent quote

Leave with a written plan, a clear estimate, and time to think. Nothing is pressured — treatment happens on your timeline.

~10 min
