New patient visit

What to expect, start to finish.

Your first visit sets the foundation for everything that follows. Here's how it goes — no surprises.

i.

A warm welcome

Arrive, meet your hygienist, and let us verify your insurance so you know exactly what's covered before we begin.

~10 min
ii.

Gentle exam & imaging

Digital X-rays and an oral exam with your dentist. We review everything with you on-screen — not at you.

~20 min
iii.

Cleaning & conversation

A thorough but gentle cleaning from your hygienist, with time to talk through what we see and any concerns you have.

~30 min
iv.

Plan & transparent quote

Leave with a written plan, a clear estimate, and time to think. Nothing is pressured — treatment happens on your timeline.

~10 min
